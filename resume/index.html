Research

Current research involves the intersection of set-theoretic topology and infinite combinatorial games. In particular, this research includes results on characterizing covering properties using limited information strategies in topological games.

Examples include Gruenhage’s W-convergence game, Gruenhage’s compact-point/compact game, Menger’s covering game, Rothberger’s covering game, and Bell’s proximal game.

Previous work involves stationary subsets of linearly ordered topological spaces and unfoldings of convex polyhedra into the plane.
