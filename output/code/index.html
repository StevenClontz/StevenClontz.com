Teloga.com

The best learning experience I’ve had so far as a developer has been working on Teloga.com. Teloga Musician Networks is a website for music programs such as university bands to better connect with their members, alumni, and recruits. We integrate a simple online registration system for events into a sophisticated membership database so that programs can have a definite source of information on their personnel.

The site is a product of Teloga, LLC, co-founded by my buddy James Dabbs and myself. In addition to the software engineering experience it’s given me, I’ve also been in charge of Teloga’s sales and support. I talk a bit more about it in my portfolio.
